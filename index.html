About the Project

Farmers Marketplace is a platform designed to empower local farmers and enable them to sell their products directly to consumers, improving market access and transparency. We aim to create a simple, user-friendly platform that connects farmers with the global marketplace, ensuring fair prices and a streamlined transaction process.

Our Features

Product Listings

Farmers can create detailed product listings with descriptions, pricing, and images, making it easy for buyers to find fresh produce.

Secure Payments

Our platform uses secure payment gateways to ensure that all transactions are safe and reliable for both farmers and buyers.

Farmer Profiles

Each farmer has a customizable profile that includes reviews, ratings, and product history to build trust with buyers.
